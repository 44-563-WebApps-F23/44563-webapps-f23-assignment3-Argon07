Favorite Food

My all-time favorite food is Paneer Tikka. There's something about the combination of fresh Paneer, perfectly seasoned veggies, and a touch of spices that I find absolutely irresistible. The variety of flavors and textures in Paneer Tikka is simply amazing. Whether it's traditional Paneer Tikka, creative rolls, or sashimi, I appreciate the artistry and taste of Paneer dishes. The balance of flavors and the aesthetic presentation make it a culinary delight that I can't get enough of.
